Equity Funds

If you want to invest in stock market but do not have the time to keep track of performing scripts, look no further. HBL Asset Management’s Balanced / Equity Funds aims to provide the investors with superior risk-adjusted returns by investing in blue-chip companies that have the potential to provide high growth and returns. Our experienced & qualified Fund Managers actively manage the equity fund portfolio so that our investors get superior risk-adjusted returns.
These funds are volatile compared to Money Market / Income Funds schemes available and are best suited for investors who have a long-term investment horizon.
