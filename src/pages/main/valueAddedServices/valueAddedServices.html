SMS Services

With our SMS service*, you can access information related to the your Zakat Status and investment account. Just send an SMS at ‘9300’ and you can check about:

Investment Value

Check your investment account balance. Type “BAL <space> <FOLIO number> and send to “9300”

Zakat Status

Want to know about your Zakat Status? Simply type “Zakat <space> <FOLIO number> and send to “9300”

Call-back

Don’t know where to invest? Just SMS ‘Invest’ to “9300″ to get a call-back from our Investment Advisor
