How To Invest

Investing with HBL Asset Management Limited has never been this easy. Simply follow the steps below to start investing with us:

  • Calculate your risk appetite by filling out our Risk Assessment Questionnaire. The result will recommend the best investment strategy you should follow.
  • Download and completely fill the HBL AMC Account Opening and Investment form, available on our website.
  • Deliver your form along with your investment cheque and required documents at our Regional Office or courier it to our Head Office.
  • Contact our Investment Facilitator at (021) 111-425-262 to understand more about the recommended investment strategy.
  • Don’t know where to invest? Just SMS ‘Invest’ to “9300″ to get a call-back from our Investment Advisor
